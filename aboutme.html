About me

Hey there! Thanks for stopping by.

I’m a UX Designer at TOPdesk Deutschland GmbH, where I design enterprise self-service and service management experiences

I studied design at the National Institute of Design, India and completed my engineering degree at Delhi Technological University. This combination continues to shape how I approach problem-solving today.

Before this, I’ve worked with companies like Thoughtworks, Wipro Technologies, and Havells India Ltd., designing for enterprise B2B products across different domains.

I enjoy working at the intersection of user needs, business goals, and technical constraints. I collaborate closely with cross-functional teams to turn complex challenges into practical, scalable solutions.

Outside of work, here are a few things I enjoy:

- I’m passionate about social impact and enjoy volunteering my time and skills with non-profit organizations. I particularly like working with children, teaching and engaging them through art and creative activities.

- I’m deeply curious about the human mind and enjoy reading about psychology and design. Authors like Edward de Bono and Don Norman are among my favourites.

- In my free time, I enjoy getting creative through doodling, sketching, and digital illustration. I often create personalized artworks as gifts for friends and family.
